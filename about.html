About the League

This is 613 Playground

Ottawa's home for competitive, community-run basketball. We bring hoopers together for league nights, playoffs and open runs — all in the 613.

Who We Are

613 Playground started with a simple idea: give Ottawa ballers a real place to compete and a community to do it with. What began as casual runs grew into an organized league with teams, standings, playoffs and a championship trophy. Season 1 crowned our first champions, and Season 2 is bringing four squads back to battle for the title.

Whether you're a seasoned hooper or just looking to get back on the court, there's a place for you here — on a league roster or at an open run. Games are filmed and shared, the competition is real, and the community is what keeps people coming back.
