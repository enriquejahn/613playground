About the League

This is 613 Playground

Ottawa's home for competitive, community-run basketball. We bring hoopers together for league nights, playoffs and open runs — all in the 613.

Who We Are

613 Playground started with a group of friends getting together to play ball. From runs at local parks to drop-ins and leagues across the city, we spent years sharing our love for the game.

Along the way, we realized it was never just about basketball & volleyball. The games gave us a reason to show up, but it was the people, friendships, and memories that kept us coming back.

What started as a few friends on the court has grown into a community where people can compete, connect, and be part of something bigger. Whether you're new to the game or have been playing for years, there's a place for you here.
