Drop-In Basketball

Open Runs

No team, no commitment — just sign up and hoop. Tuesdays are 3-on-3, Fridays are 5-on-5. All skill levels welcome.

Game Format

Tuesday · 3-on-3

  • 3 vs 3 — capped at 24 players
  • 4–6 teams, pre-made rosters by skill level
  • First half round robin, second half king's court
  • 10-minute games — scoring 3s & 2s
  • Games played to 15 or the time limit

Friday · 5-on-5

  • 5 vs 5 — capped at 20 players
  • 4 teams, pre-made rosters by skill level
  • Round robin style — guaranteed 6 games
