About the League

613 Playground is Ottawa's home for competitive, community-run basketball. Season 2 brings back four squads — the Blue Lions, Blacklipinos, Cavs and Bulls — for a full season of league play, standings and playoffs, with open drop-in runs in between. All levels welcome. Lace up.
